Our Venues

AMMA Fighting Pit

The first gym in Central London is a modern and well-equipped facility that offers a wide variety of MMA classes for all skill levels. It has top-of-the-line equipment and experienced instructors who provide a friendly and supportive atmosphere for students. The gym also offers personal training and open gym time.

AMMA Combat Zone

The second gym in East London specializes in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and is known for its tight-knit community and a focused and dedicated atmosphere. The gym offers a variety of BJJ classes, as well as MMA classes and open mat time. The instructors are experienced BJJ practitioners, and the gym has a strong competition team.

AMMA Fight Factory

The third gym in North London is a high-end facility that caters to professional fighters and serious athletes. It has top-of-the-line equipment, a wide variety of classes and training programs, and experienced coaches. The gym is known for its intense training atmosphere and reputation for producing top-level fighters. The gym is exclusive and selective and only accepts members who meet certain requirements.
